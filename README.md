Project Name :Keen Keeper

Discription :
 This is a social friend tracking website where I can see a record of who I've been connected to since I last talked to a friend and how long ago I talked to them.

 Technology :
 html, tailwindcss , react, nextjs, daisyui , reac icon , Rechart

 key feture : 
 01 . All my friends' identities are shown on the home page.

 02. Clicking on any friend's identity card will take you to a separate page with all the information about that friend.


 03. A record of how many people I have joined via TEXT, VIDEO, or CALL is shown through a chart on the status page.
